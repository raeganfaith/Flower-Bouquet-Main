About Us

Bloom Inc. is absolutely FREE for customers! That's right, the company is a 100% customer focused website which looks at making shopping an easy task for the consumer.

  • Easy and convenient
  • User-friendly
  • Allows customization of flower bouquets

Bloom Inc. was recently launched by students from INF202 which focused on creating an easy and convenient way of buying flowers for your loved ones on a special occassion or just any old day!

Do you desire anything more unique and romantic? Customize your bouquet by hand picking your own flowers and selecting the best sleeve and tie to go with it! Additionally, there are pre-made bouquets of exquisitely arranged flowers. Even better, include a greeting card with your bouquet of flowers.
