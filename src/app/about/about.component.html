About Us

Bloom Inc. aims to be your number one wingman! We're a small company dedicated in delivering the most beautiful bouquets for you to give to your loved ones. We're here to help you spread the love, one flower at a time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be people's first thought when thinking of sending flowers to someone. We intend to provide the most beautiful arrangements and delivering it right when you need it. We believe in spreading kindness and love, and we'll do what we can to help out.

Our Vision

We envision Bloom Inc. blooming the country with lots of beautiful flowers! We hope we get to see our brand get promoted by famous people and brands. Most importantly, we aim to be the bridge for many people's relationships, whether it be romantic, familial, or platonic!

Meet the Team!

Bloom Inc. was recently launched by students from INF202 which focused on creating an easy and convenient way of buying flowers for your loved ones on a special occassion or just any old day!
